Happy new year, This is not surprising given that Ho Chi Minh City is the country’s largest engine of economy, where businesses have been mushrooming leading to the development of technology and requiring a large human resource to sustain and promote that growth. According to statistics, the average salary for programmers in Ho Chi Minh City is currently maintained at 38.8 million VND/month while in Hanoi it is 34.3 million VND/month and in Da Nang it is 27.6 million VND/month. This level of income fluctuates differently depending on factors such as location, programming language, experience,… However, the above figures do not include other benefits of the company.
